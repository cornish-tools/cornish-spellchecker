A Cornish spell-checker in Standard Written Form (SWF). This spell-checker combines all words from both Revived Middle Cornish (RMC) and Revived Late Cornish (RLC).

The word list is from Akademi Kernewek (https://www.akademikernewek.org.uk/) provided by the Cornish Language Office (cornishlanguage@cornwall.gov.uk).
